### 2.3 Shared Technical Requirements

These rules apply to ALL categories:

- All submissions must include a **detailed report** covering:
  - Achievements โ key accomplishments
  - "As-Is" Situation and Implemented Changes
  - Measurable Results โ quantitative and qualitative evidence
  - Economic Value Added
  - Critical Success Factors
  - Relevant KPIs and Future Targets
  - Other Key Data and Feedback
- Applicants shall prepare a **comprehensive presentation** for the personal interview
- All evidence should be compiled **digitally in a logical, sequential manner**
- Accepted file types: **PDF, PPTX, DOCX, XLSX, JPG, PNG**

### 2.4 Do's & Don'ts (Display on every criteria form)

**Do:**
- Ensure evidence is specific, measurable, and relevant
- Present information clearly and concisely
- Compile all evidence in a logical and sequential digital format

**Don't:**
- Use the criteria as a simple checklist
- Provide general or vague evidence; include specific examples from the last two years
- Exceed the word limit specified for each criterion
- Exceed the allowed number of attachments

### 2.5 Accepted Evidence Types (Display as helper)

Letters | Minutes of Meetings | Press Evidence | Photographs | Administrative Decisions | Statistics | Survey Results | Benchmarking | Organization Structure | Reports | Membership IDs | Essays

## 9. Technical Notes

### 9.1 Dynamic Form Rendering

The criteria form (`/apply/[category]/criteria`) must be **dynamically rendered** based on category and sub-category:

```
if category === "employee":
    if sub_category in [admin, specialized, technical, customer, soldier]:
        render PATTERN_A (5 criteria, weights: 40-15-15-15-15)
    elif sub_category === "future-leader":
        render PATTERN_B (6 criteria, weights: 20-15-15-15-15-20)
    elif sub_category === "leader":
        render PATTERN_C (6 DIFFERENT criteria, weights: 20-15-15-15-15-20)

elif category === "department":
    render 4 criteria (EFQM, 200-300-200-300)

elif category === "project":
    render 3 criteria with sub-criteria tables (20-30-50)

elif category === "knowledge":
    render 10 criteria grouped: Enablers(6) + Results(4)

elif category === "green":
    render 8 criteria grouped: Enablers(5) + Results(3)
```
